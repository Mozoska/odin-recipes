Homemade Beef Jerky Recipe

Here's a simple recipe for making delicious homemade beef jerky, perfect for snacks or hiking trips.

---

Ingredients

  • 1 kg (about 2.2 lbs) lean beef (flank, top round, or bottom round), thinly sliced (about 3-5 mm or 1/8-1/4 inch thick)
  • 125 ml (about 1/2 cup) soy sauce (low sodium preferred)
  • 60 ml (about 1/4 cup) Worcestershire sauce
  • 60 ml (about 1/4 cup) teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper, freshly ground
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon liquid smoke (optional)

Instructions

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Marinating Time: 6-24 hours

Cook Time: 4-8 hours

Yield: Varies

  1. Prepare the Meat: Slice the beef thinly. Slice with the grain for tougher, chewier jerky, or against the grain for more tender jerky.
  2. Prepare the Marinade: In a large bowl, whisk together soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, teriyaki sauce, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, cayenne pepper (if desired), and liquid smoke (if desired).
  3. Marinate: Place the beef slices into a large resealable bag or shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the beef, ensuring each piece is well coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 6 hours, ideally 24 hours.
  4. Prepare for Drying: Remove the beef from the marinade and drain any excess liquid. Pat the beef dry thoroughly with paper towels. This step is crucial for proper drying.
  5. Oven Drying Method: Preheat your oven to the lowest possible temperature (ideally 70-80째C / 160-175째F). Arrange the beef slices in a single layer on wire racks placed over baking sheets, ensuring they don't overlap. Leave the oven door slightly ajar to allow moisture to escape. Dry for 4 to 8 hours, or until the jerky is dry but still pliable.
  6. Dehydrator Method: Follow your dehydrator's instructions. Typically, the temperature is set between 60-70째C (140-160째F) for 4-8 hours.
  7. Cooling and Storage: Let the jerky cool completely. Store in an airtight container at room temperature or in the refrigerator.

Tips

  • For more intense flavor, add spices like ground ginger, coriander, or red pepper flakes.
  • Liquid smoke provides a smoky flavor without needing a smoker.
  • Drying time varies depending on the thickness of the beef slices and your oven or dehydrator.
